Got Questions? We can help

Everything you need to know about the Agri Loans. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Please chat to our team.

Who is eligible for an Agri Loan?
Farmers, agribusiness owners, and agricultural cooperatives with valid farming operations and necessary documentation are eligible for Agri Loans.
How much can I borrow?
You can borrow from MWK 10,000 up to MWK 1,000,000 depending on your farming needs, scale of operation, and repayment capacity.
What can the loan be used for?
Agri Loans can be used for seeds, fertilizers, equipment, machinery, irrigation systems, livestock, feed, storage facilities, and other farming-related expenses.
How long does it take to process the loan?
Loan processing typically takes 24-48 hours after submission of all required documents.
What are the repayment terms?
Repayment terms range from 6 to 60 months, with flexibility to align with your farming cycles and harvest seasons.
