General Requirements

What you need to apply for a loan

Eligibility Criteria

  • Malawian citizen or resident
  • 18-65 years of age
  • Valid national ID or passport
  • Proof of regular income
  • Good credit history (for certain loans)
  • Active bank account
  • Valid mobile phone number

Required Documents

  • National ID/Passport (copy)
  • Recent passport photo
  • Proof of income (payslips, bank statements)
  • Proof of residence (utility bill, rental agreement)
  • Business registration documents (for business loans)
  • Collateral documents (for secured loans)
  • Employer confirmation letter (for civil servants)
