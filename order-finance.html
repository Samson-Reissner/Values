What is Order Finance?

Order Finance is designed for businesses that receive large purchase orders but lack the upfront capital to deliver. With Value Loans, you get quick access to funds that cover inventory, raw materials, or services, so you can confidently fulfil every order.

Who Can Apply?

Order Finance is available to businesses that meet the following:

  • Registered business with MRA
  • Operating for at least 12 months
  • Demonstrable monthly revenue
  • Valid NRC of directors
  • Ability to provide acceptable collateral
Apply Now

Required Documents

To complete your application, you'll need:

  • Last 6 months financial statements
  • Proof of past fulfilled orders with similar buyers
  • Collateral worth at least 50% Loan-to-Value (LTV)
  • Purchase order details: buyer name, amount, and delivery time
  • Certificate of Incorporation

Got Questions? We can help

What is the minimum tenure for Order Finance?
The minimum tenure for Order Finance is 62 days, designed to accommodate typical purchase order fulfillment cycles.
How much financing can I access?
Financing amounts are based on your purchase order value, business capacity, and collateral, typically ranging from MWK 50,000 to MWK 5,000,000.
How fast is approval?
Approval typically takes 24 hours after submission of all required documents and purchase order verification.
Do I need collateral?
Yes, Order Finance requires collateral worth at least 50% of the loan value to secure the financing.
What's the interest rate?
Value Loans offers competitive business-friendly interest rates designed to keep financing affordable.
