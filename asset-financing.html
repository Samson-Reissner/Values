What is Asset Financing?

Asset financing allows your business to purchase equipment, vehicles, or technology with flexible repayment terms. Instead of paying large sums upfront, you spread costs over time while keeping your cash flow intact.

Got Questions? We can help

Everything you need to know about the Asset Financing. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Please chat to our team.

How much can I borrow?
You can borrow from MWK 50,000 to MWK 5,000,000 depending on your business needs, financial capacity, and the asset being financed.
How long are the repayment terms?
Repayment terms range from 12 to 60 months, giving you flexibility to match your business cash flow.
Do I need collateral?
The asset being financed typically serves as collateral for the loan, making it easier to secure financing.
How long is the repayment period?
Repayment periods range from 12 to 60 months, allowing you to choose a term that suits your business revenue cycles.
