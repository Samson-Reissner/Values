You may qualify if you:

  • Are 18 years or older
  • Have a regular monthly income (public or private sector)
  • Can provide collateral such as a vehicle, land title, or valuable asset
  • Complete full Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements

Got Questions? We can help

Everything you need to know about the Collateral Backed Loan. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Please chat to our team.

What types of collateral can I use?
You can use various assets including vehicles, land titles, property documents, or other valuable assets that can be properly evaluated and documented.
What happens if I miss a payment?
We recommend contacting our support team immediately if you anticipate payment difficulties. We offer flexible solutions to help you manage your repayments.
How fast can I get the loan?
Once approved, funds are typically disbursed within 24-48 hours to your preferred account or mobile wallet.
How fast is the approval process?
Approval typically takes 24-48 hours after submission of all required documents and collateral evaluation.
