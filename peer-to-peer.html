How Peer-to-Peer Lending Works

A simple, transparent, and secure way to borrow or lend money through our verified user marketplace.

1

Register & Verify

Only verified users can lend or borrow. Create an account, provide basic KYC details, and verify your phone number.

2

Choose to Borrow or Lend

Borrowers can request an amount. Lenders can browse borrower profiles, repayment capacity, and risk levels.

3

Send or Receive Funds

When a lender accepts a request, they send the funds directly to the borrower. A screenshot/confirmation of payment is uploaded in the system.
