Who We Are

VALUE is an AI-powered neobank providing digital loans, payments, and investments across the Country.

Since our founding in 2016, we have grown from a lending platform into a comprehensive neobank with a Pan-African strategy focused on serving SMEs and individuals.

As one of the first companies approved by the Security Exchange Commission (SEC) in Malawi to provide investments through peer-to-peer lending, we're proud to be at the forefront of financial innovation in Africa.
