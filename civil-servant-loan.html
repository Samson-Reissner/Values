Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for a Civil Service Loan, you must:

  • Have been in employment for 6 months
  • Be over 18 years of age
  • Provide a valid NRC
  • Consent to salary-based deductions
  • Have completed Value Loans' simple KYC process

Got Questions? We can help

Everything you need to know about the Civil Service Loan. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Please chat to our team.

How much can I borrow as a civil servant?
You can borrow from MWK 500 to MWK 250,000 depending on your salary level and employment duration.
How are repayments made?
Repayments are made through direct salary deductions, making it convenient and hassle-free.
How fast is the approval process?
Approval typically takes 24-48 hours after submission of all required documents.
Do I need collateral to get this loan?
No, Civil Service Loans are unsecured and backed by your employment status.
