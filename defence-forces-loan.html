Got Questions? We can help

Everything you need to know about the Malawi Defence Forces Loan. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Please chat to our team.

How long does loan approval take?
Loan approval typically takes 24-48 hours after submission of all required documents.
Is collateral required?
No, Malawi Defence Forces Loans are unsecured and backed by your employment status and salary.
What is the loan term?
Loan terms range from 3 to 36 months, giving you flexibility to choose what works best for your financial situation.
How do I receive the funds?
Once approved, funds are disbursed directly to your bank account within 24-48 hours.
